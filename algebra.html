Algebra

Learn Algebra vector concepts, operations and solved examples ✅

Overview

Definition: Algebra is the branch of mathematics that uses symbols and letters to represent numbers and quantities in formulas and equations.

Key Concepts & Formulas:

1. Polynomials: \( P(x) = a_nx^n + a_{n-1}x^{n-1} + ... + a_0 \)
Degree of polynomial = highest power of \( x \)

2. Factorization Formulas:
• Difference of squares: \( a^2 - b^2 = (a-b)(a+b) \)
• Sum of cubes: \( a^3 + b^3 = (a+b)(a^2 - ab + b^2) \)
• Difference of cubes: \( a^3 - b^3 = (a-b)(a^2 + ab + b^2) \)
• Perfect square trinomial: \( a^2 + 2ab + b^2 = (a+b)^2, \; a^2 - 2ab + b^2 = (a-b)^2 \)

3. Indices & Exponents:
• \( a^m \cdot a^n = a^{m+n} \)
• \( \frac{a^m}{a^n} = a^{m-n} \)
• \( (a^m)^n = a^{mn} \)
• \( a^{-n} = \frac{1}{a^n} \)

4. Surds & Radicals:
• \( \sqrt{a \cdot b} = \sqrt{a} \cdot \sqrt{b} \)
• \( \sqrt{\frac{a}{b}} = \frac{\sqrt{a}}{\sqrt{b}} \)
• Rationalizing denominator: \( \frac{1}{\sqrt{a}} = \frac{\sqrt{a}}{a} \)

5. Logarithms:
• \( \log_a(xy) = \log_a x + \log_a y \)
• \( \log_a(x/y) = \log_a x - \log_a y \)
• \( \log_a(x^n) = n \log_a x \)
• \( a^{\log_a x} = x \)

6. Sequences & Series:
• Arithmetic progression (AP): \( a_n = a + (n-1)d \), \( S_n = \frac{n}{2}[2a + (n-1)d] \)
• Geometric progression (GP): \( a_n = a r^{n-1} \), \( S_n = a \frac{r^n - 1}{r-1} \) (r ≠ 1)

7. Matrices & Determinants:
See matrix and determinant formulas in previous template section.

Examples

Example 1: Simplifying Polynomials

Given: \( 5x^3 + 2x^2 - 3x^3 + x^2 \)
Solution: Combine like terms: \( (5x^3-3x^3) + (2x^2 + x^2) = 2x^3 + 3x^2 \)

Example 2: Factorization

Given: \( x^3 + 27 \)
Solution: Sum of cubes: \( x^3 + 3^3 = (x+3)(x^2 - 3x + 9) \)

Example 3: Using Indices

Given: Simplify \( x^4 \cdot x^{-6} \)
Solution: \( x^{4+(-6)} = x^{-2} = 1/x^2 \)

Example 4: Simplifying Surds

Given: \( \sqrt{200} \)
Solution: \( \sqrt{200} = \sqrt{100 \cdot 2} = 10\sqrt{2} \)

Example 5: Logarithms

Given: \( \log_2 8 \)
Solution: Since \( 2^3 = 8 \), \( \log_2 8 = 3 \)

Example 6: AP & GP

AP: Find 5th term of AP: 2,5,8,...
\( a=2, d=3, n=5 \)
\( a_5 = a + (n-1)d = 2 + 4*3 = 14 \)

GP: Find 4th term of GP: 3,6,12,...
\( a=3, r=2, n=4 \)
\( a_4 = a r^{n-1} = 3*2^3 = 24 \)
